About Us

Welcome to Lucky Shrub, your trusted partner in creating breathtaking outdoor and indoor spaces. Located in the heart of Tucson, Arizona, we specialize in garden design and creation, landscaping, and garden maintenance, with a mission to transform any space into a serene oasis you'll be proud to call your own.
Founded by husband-and-wife duo Jason and Maria, Lucky Shrub combines passion and expertise to deliver exceptional results. Jason, our skilled garden architect, brings your vision to life with innovative designs and oversees a dedicated team of landscapers. Maria ensures every project shines, managing our marketing and curating a beautiful plant nursery featuring a wide range of indoor and outdoor plants.
